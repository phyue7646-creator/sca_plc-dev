# Diploma Descriptions

This file contains descriptions of all diplomas available for the SCA Sustainability Project. Edit this file to update or add diploma information. Diplomas are listed under `## ` (level 2) headings — please keep that format so the app can read them correctly.

## Diploma in Accountancy & Finance

This comprehensive programme brings together accountancy, finance, sustainability and technology, equipping you with the skills to influence businesses, transform industries, and lead with purpose. Accountancy and finance today is more than just numbers — it's about driving growth, shaping key decisions, and creating lasting value. As the world rises to the challenge of climate change, a powerful new frontier is emerging where accountancy and finance are fuelling a sustainable future. With Singapore leading this global transformation, students will be ready to take the lead and make their mark.

## Diploma in Business

The Diploma in Business offers a dynamic and future-oriented curriculum designed to cater to the evolving demands of industries in Singapore and the broader region. Through personalised learning experiences, students are equipped with multidisciplinary and transferable skill sets necessary to excel in a rapidly changing workforce and thrive in diverse roles and industries. Through collaborations with industry partners such as Enterprise Singapore, the National Heritage Board, PERSOL, SAP, SGTech, the Singapore National Employers Federation, the Society for Human Resource Management, and Workato, students benefit from professional development opportunities and industry certifications, enhancing their learning with real-world insights and connections.

## Diploma in Law and Management

The original polytechnic law diploma in Singapore, this course equips students with relevant knowledge and hands-on skills for a career in the legal industry as an allied legal professional.

Theoretical and procedural law subjects are built upon a strong foundation in business — communication skills, management, accounting, economics, technology, and marketing. Lifelong skills such as innovation, entrepreneurship, and a global mindset are part of the training. Students are also trained to use the latest Legal Technology applications, ensuring they're adept at the technology that is transforming legal practice.

With a multidisciplinary approach led by lecturers with years of industry experience, the course equips students with the skills to comprehensively manage complex issues and add value to the legal profession. The course is strongly supported by key industry partners such as the Singapore Academy of Law and the Law Society of Singapore. An 18-week internship provides realistic on-the-job training with law firms and legal departments in public and private organisations.

## Diploma in Chemical Engineering

Chemical engineers play a crucial role in making clean energy, reducing waste, creating eco-friendly chemicals, ensuring clean water, and developing the formulation and processing of drugs and medicines. Their diverse contributions safeguard the environment and make significant strides in addressing global challenges such as poverty and disease.

In this multidisciplinary field of mathematics, chemistry, physics, biology and engineering, students discover how raw materials are processed to create the products used in daily life. These include fuels, paints, biodegradable plastics, medicines, cosmetics, personal care products, food, and water. Education goes beyond classrooms and laboratories — students undergo work-based learning in state-of-the-art facilities and a six-month internship.

In Year 3, students choose a specialisation in Applied Chemistry, Sustainable Energy & Chemical Production, Pharmaceuticals & Biologics Manufacturing, Semiconductor Technology, or Technical Sales & Marketing. The holistic training enables students to embark on careers in petrochemical, speciality chemical, pharmaceutical, energy, semiconductor, environment, and water industries.

## Diploma in Veterinary Technology

This comprehensive course integrates theoretical knowledge with hands-on practice, empowering students to become skilled veterinary technicians. For students with aspirations in marine science or aquaculture, this programme also lays a solid foundation for those fields.

Under the guidance of an expert team, students receive specialised training in veterinary care, mastering essential techniques such as animal husbandry, anaesthesia monitoring, and diagnostic testing at the fully equipped and licensed TP Animal Clinic & Wellness facility. The broad-based and well-structured curriculum cultivates expertise in caring for aquatic animals and pets.

With growing awareness about animal welfare issues and the pursuit of advancing medical solutions for various ailments, students gain a scientific grasp of the applications of laboratory animal science, with an emphasis on responsible and humane animal care practices.

## Diploma in Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics

Students learn to protect, secure and defend computing and information systems, applications, and networks. Should a cybersecurity breach occur, students are able to retrieve and reconstruct evidence, investigate the cause, and advise how to prevent further attacks.

Students also gain hands-on training at the Temasek Advanced Learning, Nurturing & Testing Lab — a cyber-range set up in collaboration with the Ministry of Home Affairs — and have the opportunity to investigate live malware samples in the Malware Analysis Centre.

## Diploma in Information Technology

In this course, students acquire very strong programming and software development skills, and learn to incorporate AI, data analytics, and other technologies to enhance the applications they develop. Students get the opportunity to specialise in areas such as Financial Technologies (Fintech) through elective subjects and gain employment in the banking and financial services sectors.

A unique opportunity is offered through Industry Practice elective subjects, which students can take while working at an internship company in their 3rd year. This provides a chance to acquire strong and deep skills at the workplace and prepares students well to secure future employment.

The course has very strong industry links with DBS and Deloitte, where students are mentored by industry professionals and can undertake year-long internships with these esteemed companies in Year 3.
